Hello! I'm Fiona - a fun-size, food-loving technical recruiter at Lookahead Search based in Sydney, Australia. I was a front-end developer for a long time, but decided to try something new while still remain in the tech industry.

I love CSS, I love talking to people about front-end and I love learning any new things in this ever-changing web world. I'm also passionate about usability and accessibility. We need to think about our users y'all!

As a front-end developer, I have a lot of experience in architecting large scale front-end, creating modular CSS and setting front-end standards.

As a technical recruiter now, I want to connect great people with great companies. Hopefully help people find their dream jobs!

I'm also one of the organisers for SydCSS and CSSConf 2014. I really love being involved in the community!

Outside of web, you can find me planning what to eat for my next meal and daydreaming about the street food in Vietnam.
